Why purse Double Degrees? During my years at UCF, I've found that majoring in both Web & Graphic Design aligns with my career goals the best. I aim to bridge the gap between aesthetics and useability through my work. Learning Web Design enhances my knowledge and communication skills with others in development. I can better meet project strategic goals through comprehension of the scope and workflow from both sides as a designer and developer. As a graphic design student, creativity is fostered through active use of different mediums, as well as ways to visually appeal information to others.

While utilizing design is most fulfilling to me through engagement, useability, and problem-solving, I do enjoy other types of art for storytelling purposes! I currently have side projects for Game Design and an Interactive Novel I build upon in down times.
